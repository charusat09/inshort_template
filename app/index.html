'Mohenjo Daro' earns ₹30.54 crore in 1st weekend
short by Prashanti Moktan / 02:25 pm on 15 Aug 2016,Monday
The Hrithik Roshan and Pooja Hegde starrer 'Mohenjo Daro' has earned ₹30.54 crore in its opening weekend. As per Bollywood trade analyst Taran Adarsh, it collected ₹8.87 crore on Friday, ₹9.60 crore on Saturday and ₹12.07 crore on Sunday. Directed by Ashutosh Gowariker, the film is set in 2016 BC during the Indus Valley civilization.

Dipa fought elbow injury during gymnastics final
short by Dishant Sharma / 02:25 pm on 15 Aug 2016,Monday
India's first ever gymnast at the Olympics, Dipa Karmakar was nursing an elbow injury during the event's final on Sunday. The 23-year old gymnast, who finished 4th in the event, had injured her elbow during training, a day prior to the event's final. "The number of injuries​ Dipa has played with is mind boggling", Dipa’s physiotherapist Mir Sajid said.
